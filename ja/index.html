逆転裁判とは

2001年10月にゲームボーイアドバンス用ソフトとして発売された、
初の“法廷バトル”ゲーム『逆転裁判』。
新米弁護士となって無実の罪を着せられた依頼人を救うべく、
事件の真相を暴いていく。

The game follows a rookie attorney as he fights to save his clients in court by uncovering the truth behind each case.

Its unique gameplay of taking hopeless situations and turning them around, as well as entertaining mysteries featuring a cast of colorful characters, made waves among players that continue to this day.

In addition to the mainline games, there are two additional sub-series : Ace Attorney Investigations, which features a prosecutor as the main character, and The Great Ace Attorney, which is set in Victorian era London and Meiji Japan.
